# Job Analyzed

**Company:** TechNova Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

**Role:** Software Development Intern

**Location:** Bengaluru, India (Hybrid)

**Duration:** 6 Months

**Stipend:** ₹25,000/month

---

# Risk Analysis Summary

**Overall Risk Score:** **22/100**

**Final Verdict:** ✅ Apply with Caution – Learning Positive

The role appears to be a good opportunity for students looking to gain practical software development experience. Most concerns are related to missing information rather than major warning signs.

---

# Top Red Flags

- Hybrid work arrangement is not clearly defined.
- PPO evaluation criteria are not specified.
- Hiring process and interview rounds are not explained.
- Mid-sized company with limited publicly available information.

---

# Positive Signals

- Transparent stipend of ₹25,000/month.
- Realistic requirements suitable for interns.
- Modern technology stack (React, Node.js, MongoDB, REST APIs).
- Mentorship from experienced engineers.
- Structured internship program with learning opportunities.
- No unrealistic experience requirements.
- No toxic workplace language or "hustle culture" indicators.

---

# Smart Interview Questions

1. Can you clarify the hybrid work policy and office attendance expectations?
2. How is PPO eligibility evaluated during the internship?
3. What projects will interns primarily work on?
4. What does the interview and selection process look like?
5. How many interns typically receive PPOs after completing the internship?

---

# Key Learnings

- Always evaluate a company before applying rather than focusing only on the role.
- Missing information in a job description can indicate areas to clarify during interviews.
- Transparent stipend, mentorship, and realistic expectations are strong positive indicators.
- Asking thoughtful interview questions helps avoid misunderstandings later.
- AI can quickly identify hidden risks and save time during the job search process.
