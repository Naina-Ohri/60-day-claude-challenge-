# Custom Skill

**Skill Name:** stock-fundamental-research

**Purpose:**
A reusable Claude Custom Skill designed to analyze Indian and global listed companies using business fundamentals, valuation metrics, financial statements, ownership trends, competitive advantages, risks, and growth prospects while avoiding direct investment recommendations.

---

# Stock Analyzed

**Company:** Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)

---

# Generated Report

The generated report included:

* Company overview
* Market capitalization and valuation metrics
* Revenue and profit analysis
* ROE, ROCE and Debt analysis
* Business strengths and competitive advantages
* Financial health assessment
* Risk factors
* Peer comparison
* Fundamental quality assessment
* Charts and visual summaries
* Source references for key financial data

---

# Observations

* Successfully created a reusable Claude Custom Skill.
* The skill could be invoked without re-entering the complete prompt.
* Claude generated a structured and detailed stock research report.
* Financial metrics, charts, and business analysis were presented in a well-organized format.
* The report remained evidence-based and avoided giving direct buy, sell, or hold recommendations.

---

# Key Learnings

* Custom Skills eliminate repetitive prompting for recurring workflows.
* Well-structured instructions produce more consistent AI outputs.
* Reusable workflows significantly improve productivity.
* AI-generated financial research should always be verified using official financial sources before making investment decisions.

---

# Conclusion

Day 16 demonstrated how Claude Custom Skills can convert lengthy prompts into reusable AI workflows. Creating a permanent stock research skill made generating detailed, structured, and repeatable company analyses much faster and more efficient.
