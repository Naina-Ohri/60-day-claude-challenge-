# Day 19 – Football Intelligence Hub

## Overview

For Day 19 of the 60 Days of Claude Challenge, I used Claude to analyze a football dataset and generate a Football Intelligence Hub. The workflow included World Cup predictions, a Football IQ assessment, a Messi vs Ronaldo personality analysis, and a final Football Intelligence Profile.

## Generated Outputs

### FIFA World Cup 2026 Prediction Report
- AI-generated tournament prediction
- Predicted winner and runner-up
- Dark horse nation
- Players to watch
- Confidence scores with supporting reasoning.

### Football IQ Assessment
- Football knowledge evaluation
- Football Awareness Score
- Fan classification
- Strengths and improvement areas.

### Messi vs Ronaldo Personality Match
- Personality compatibility analysis
- Football personality archetype
- Recommended players, clubs, national teams, and rivalries.

### Football Intelligence Profile
- Combined report containing predictions, quiz results, personality assessment, and personalized football insights.
