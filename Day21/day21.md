## Privacy Insights

The dashboard analyzed the provided digital services and estimated the overall digital footprint.

Key observations included:

- Multiple applications belonging to the same parent companies increase ecosystem concentration.
- Social media, messaging, shopping, and payment platforms collectively contribute to a larger tracking surface.
- The dashboard clearly separated **Facts** from **Estimates** and avoided unsupported assumptions.
- Privacy recommendations focused on reducing exposure through better account management and privacy settings.
