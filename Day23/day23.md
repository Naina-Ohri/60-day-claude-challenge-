# Day 23 – Customer & MVP Blueprint with Claude

## Overview

For Day 23 of the #60DayClaudeChallenge, I used Claude as a Startup Product Manager and Customer Research Expert to transform my startup validation into a structured Customer & MVP Blueprint.

Building on the previous day's startup validation for **InterviewAce AI**, the report focused on understanding the ideal customer, validating pain points, defining the MVP, prioritizing features, identifying risks, and creating an execution roadmap before product development.

---

## Customer Insights

- Identified engineering students and fresh graduates as the primary target audience.
- Mapped the complete customer journey from awareness to retention.
- Highlighted the biggest pain points in interview preparation and placement readiness.
- Identified buying triggers and common objections before purchase.

---

## MVP Recommendations

The report recommended prioritizing an MVP focused on solving the core customer problem before adding advanced AI features.

Key priorities included:

- AI Resume Review
- Mock Interview Practice
- ATS Optimization
- Personalized Learning Roadmap

It also recommended postponing non-essential features until customer demand is validated.

---

## Key Learnings

- Customer understanding is more valuable than adding unnecessary features.
- A focused MVP helps validate assumptions faster while reducing development effort.
- MoSCoW prioritization provides a clear roadmap for deciding what to build first.
- AI can generate product strategy documents that resemble professional startup consulting reports.
