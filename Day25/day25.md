# Day 25 – AI Shark Tank Simulator

## Overview
Built an interactive AI Shark Tank Simulator that evaluates startup ideas through multiple AI investor perspectives. The application simulates a startup pitch, investor cross-examination, startup scoring, valuation, funding recommendation, and investment decision in a modern browser-based experience.

- Interactive startup pitch submission
- Four AI investor personas:

  - Founder

  - Angel Investor
- Dynamic investor questions
- Startup evaluation scorecard
- Investment recommendation
- Suggested valuation and funding analysis
- Professional Shark Tank inspired interface
- Fully browser-based HTML application

---

## Startup Evaluation Results

The simulator analyzed the startup idea across multiple business dimensions including:

- Market Potential
- Innovation
- Business Model
- Execution Capability
- Investment Worthiness

It generated a complete investment scorecard, valuation estimate, funding recommendation, and detailed reasoning behind the final decision.

---

## Investment Decision

The AI investors reviewed the startup pitch, questioned key business assumptions, evaluated the responses, and produced a final investment outcome along with supporting analysis.

---

## Key Learnings

- Investors evaluate execution just as much as the idea itself.
- Strong market validation is essential before seeking funding.
- Revenue models and scalability significantly influence investment decisions.
- Customer value proposition must be communicated clearly.
- AI can effectively simulate real-world investor feedback and startup evaluation workflows.
