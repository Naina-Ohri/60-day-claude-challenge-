PATIENT LANE
1. Patient Intake
Patient visits provider with a health need.
Awaiting Outcome
Patient waits while provider & payer process the request.
PROVIDER LANE
2. Medical Necessity Review
Clinician documents why the service is medically necessary.
3. PA Document Collection
Gather chart notes, imaging, labs & forms required by payer.
4. Submit to Payer
Provider submits PA request via portal / fax / EDI 278.
PAYER LANE
5. Payer Utilization Review
Payer's UM team checks criteria (e.g., MCG / InterQual).
6. Determination
Approval, Pend (more info needed), or Denial.
