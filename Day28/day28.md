## 📖 Key Learnings

- Hospital admissions require coordination between providers, insurance teams, nursing staff, utilization review, and case managers.
- Prior Authorization status has a major impact on admission readiness.
- Complete physician documentation significantly improves workflow efficiency.
- Clinical risk is weighted more heavily for emergency and ICU admissions.
- Utilization Review teams use InterQual and Milliman criteria to determine medical necessity.
- Care coordination helps reduce delays and improve patient outcomes.

---

## 💡 Reflection

This project helped me understand how multiple administrative and clinical teams collaborate before admitting a patient. Even when clinical care is urgent, documentation, insurance verification, prior authorization, and utilization review play an essential role in ensuring a smooth admission process.
