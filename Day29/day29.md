# Day 29 – Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab

## 📌 Challenge

Built an interactive **Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab** using Claude.

This simulation places the player in the role of a **Supply Chain Leader** responsible for guiding an organization through a major supply chain disruption. The simulator covers crisis management, supplier negotiations, executive decision-making, AI modernization strategies, and final business performance evaluation.

# 🧠 Key Learnings

- Every supply chain disruption requires balancing **cost, speed, trust, and operational risk**.
- Supplier diversification significantly reduces dependency on a single vendor.
- Negotiation decisions impact long-term supplier relationships as much as immediate business outcomes.
- Executive communication should be transparent, timely, and fact-based during crises.
- AI tools such as supplier risk monitoring and logistics optimization improve organizational resilience.
- Strategic planning and data-driven decisions are more effective than reacting impulsively during disruptions.
