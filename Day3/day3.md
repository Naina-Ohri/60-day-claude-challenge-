# Day 3 - Role-Based Prompting

## Question Used

How can I prepare for software engineering internships in the next 6 months?

## Without Role Prompt

Prompt:
How can I prepare for software engineering internships in the next 6 months?

## Founder Persona

Prompt:
You are a startup founder who has hired interns and fresh graduates.

How can I prepare for software engineering internships in the next 6 months?

## Developer Persona

Prompt:
You are a senior software engineer mentoring a 3rd-year B.Tech IT student.

How can I prepare for software engineering internships in the next 6 months?

## Key Learnings

- Role-Based Prompting changes the perspective of AI responses.
- Different roles focus on different priorities.
- Founder personas emphasize initiative, projects, and impact.
- Developer personas emphasize technical skills and interview preparation.
- Role prompts produce more relevant and specialized outputs.
