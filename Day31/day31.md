# Day 31 – AI Supply Chain Control Tower

## Challenge
Built an interactive **AI Supply Chain Control Tower** simulation using Claude.

This project simulates the role of a **Head of Operations**, where real-time supply chain disruptions must be managed while balancing operational KPIs, customer satisfaction, inventory health, transportation efficiency, and overall business performance.

## Simulation Summary

During the simulation I acted as the **Head of Operations** and managed live supply chain disruptions through an enterprise-style control tower.

The simulation included:

- Monitoring live operational KPIs
- Prioritizing critical alerts
- Choosing corrective operational actions
- Managing multiple disruptions simultaneously
- Reviewing final operational performance

---

## Key Features

- Premium dark enterprise dashboard
- Live KPI monitoring
- Random operational alerts
- Multiple decision paths
- Countdown timer
- Pause functionality
- Help / Instructions panel
- Performance grading
- Replay option
- Fully offline single-file HTML application

---

## Key Learnings

- Supply Chain Control Towers provide centralized visibility across operations.
- Operational decisions directly impact customer satisfaction, costs, inventory, and revenue.
- Prioritizing high-impact alerts improves overall business performance.
- Delayed or incorrect decisions can create cascading operational risks.
- Monitoring KPIs continuously enables faster and more informed decision-making.
- Enterprise dashboards help operations teams react quickly during disruptions.
