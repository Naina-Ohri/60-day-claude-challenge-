## Investigation Summary

Completed an interactive marketing investigation by analyzing campaign evidence and identifying the root cause behind poor campaign performance.

### Activities Completed

- Selected a preferred theme.
- Accepted a marketing investigation case.
- Examined the Investigation Board.
- Reviewed campaign objectives, budget allocation, campaign metrics, customer feedback, and social media performance.
- Analyzed supporting clues.
- Identified the primary marketing mistake.
- Reviewed the expert explanation.
- Completed the final Marketing Learning Report.

---

## Solved Case

**Company:** NimbusCloud SaaS

### Primary Marketing Mistake

A long and technically flawed demo request form created unnecessary friction, causing interested prospects to abandon the conversion process before completing their demo request.

---

## Final Results

**Investigation Score:** **100%**

---

## Key Learnings

- High engagement does not always translate into high conversions.
- Customer feedback often reveals problems that analytics alone cannot explain.
- Every stage of the marketing funnel should be optimized, not just traffic generation.
- A complicated or broken conversion process can significantly reduce campaign performance.
- Evidence-based decision making leads to better marketing strategies.
- Small user experience improvements can have a major impact on conversion rates.
