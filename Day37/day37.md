## Key Learnings
- Ownership should be assigned to the person or team closest to the problem.
- Effective delegation ensures work reaches the right people without unnecessary bottlenecks.
- Real workplace challenges are solved through collaboration rather than individual effort.
- Clear workflows improve communication, accountability, and efficiency across teams.
- Organizations function best when responsibilities and hand-offs are clearly defined.
