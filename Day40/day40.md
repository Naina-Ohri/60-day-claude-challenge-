# Day 40 – AI Assistant Builder

## Overview
Built a premium AI Study Assistant that helps students organize study sessions, ask questions, and receive AI-powered guidance through a clean and modern interface. The application was designed with a purpose-built UI, production-style system prompt, and documentation panel explaining the design process.

## Key Learnings

- Learned how an AI product is designed around a specific user problem instead of being a generic chatbot.
- Understood the importance of production-quality system prompts in defining an assistant's behavior, constraints, and response format.
- Explored how purpose-built UI/UX improves the overall user experience for AI applications.
- Learned how documentation helps explain prompt engineering decisions and future extensibility of an AI assistant.
- Gained experience creating a complete AI product workflow from ideation to interface design.
