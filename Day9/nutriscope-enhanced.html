Info & Sources
Disclaimer, methodology, and nutritional references
📋 Educational Disclaimer
NutriScope is designed for educational purposes only and is intended to help users learn about nutrition concepts and dietary patterns.

❌ Not medical advice: This application does not constitute medical, nutritional, or clinical dietary advice.
❌ Not a diagnostic tool: NutriScope cannot diagnose nutritional deficiencies, diseases, or medical conditions.
✅ Consult a professional: Always consult a registered dietitian, nutritionist, or healthcare professional for personalized dietary guidance, medical nutrition therapy, or treatment of any health condition.

Nutritional data is approximate and based on standard reference values. Individual needs vary based on health status, medications, and other factors not captured here.
📚 Nutrition References & Sources
🌐
USDA FoodData Central
Primary database for food nutrient composition. Provides per-100g nutritional profiles used in this application. fdc.nal.usda.gov
🇮🇳
ICMR — Indian Council of Medical Research
Nutrient Requirements & Recommended Dietary Allowances for Indians. Used for micronutrient targets (Iron, Calcium, Folate, etc.) relevant to the Indian population. icmr.gov.in
🌍
WHO — World Health Organization
Global nutrition guidelines, dietary reference intakes, and nutrient deficiency standards. Used for Vitamin D, B12, and micronutrient benchmarks. who.int/nutrition
📖
Mifflin-St Jeor Equation
Used for BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) calculation. Widely accepted as the most accurate equation for estimating resting energy expenditure.
🏥
NIN — National Institute of Nutrition, India
Nutritive Value of Indian Foods publication. Used for Indian-specific food nutritional data including regional staples.
⚙️ Methodology
Energy Target: Calculated using Mifflin-St Jeor BMR × Activity Factor (TDEE)
Protein Target: 1.6g per kg body weight (moderate activity standard)
Fat Target: 25% of TDEE ÷ 9 kcal/g
Carb Target: Remaining calories after protein & fat allocation
Micronutrients: Based on ICMR RDA values, adjusted for gender
Nutritional Data: Per 100g values from USDA FoodData Central & NIN India
